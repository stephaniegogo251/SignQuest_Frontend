About SignQuest

SignQuest is a web-based gamified learning platform for learning American Sign Language and Emirati Sign Language. This website was designed to be accessible to all currently supports 20 signs for both sign languages, including both words and phrases commonly used in real-world scenarios. SignQuest is currently being developed and will soon support fingerspelling as well as more phrases for both sign languages! arrow_outward

Thank You

This project would not have been possible without the help & support of my project supervisor, the users who participated in the initial testing as well as my friends and family members!

Helpful Resources

To learn more ASL signs you can visit Bill Vicars channel, and for ESL, refer to ESL Zayed channel & the Official ESL Dictionary provided by the Zayed Authority for People of Determination.

Data Privacy

This platform values user privacy and will not record/save your videos. It will only extract numerical data for measuring sign accuracy and storing your results but not actual video data.
